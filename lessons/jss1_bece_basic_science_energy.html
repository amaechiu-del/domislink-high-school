Introduction to Energy

Energy is the ability to do work. It comes in various forms, such as thermal, kinetic, potential, electrical, and chemical energy.

Forms of Energy

  • Thermal Energy: the energy of heat
  • Kinetic Energy: the energy of motion
  • Potential Energy: stored energy
  • Electrical Energy: the energy of moving charges
  • Chemical Energy: the energy stored in chemical bonds

Nigerian Examples of Energy

In Nigeria, energy is used in various ways. For example, the Kainji Dam uses potential energy to generate electrical energy. Additionally, the Nigeria LNG plant in Bonny uses chemical energy from natural gas to produce liquefied natural gas.

Fun Fact

Did you know that the human body is a great example of energy conversion? The food we eat (chemical energy) is converted into kinetic energy, which allows us to move and perform various activities.
