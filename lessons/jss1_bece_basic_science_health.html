Introduction to Health

Health is a state of complete physical, mental, and social well-being, and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity.

The following are key aspects of health:

  • Physical health: This refers to the proper functioning of the body and its systems.
  • Mental health: This refers to a person's emotional and psychological well-being.
  • Social health: This refers to a person's ability to interact and function within their community and society.

Examples of healthy practices in Nigeria include:

  • The National Health Insurance Scheme (NHIS) which provides access to healthcare for millions of Nigerians.
  • The "Clean Nigeria: Use the Toilet" campaign which aims to improve sanitation and hygiene across the country.

Fun Fact

Did you know that regular exercise can reduce the risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease, diabetes, and some cancers?
