Introduction to Living Things

All living things are made up of cells and have the ability to grow, reproduce, and respond to their environment.

Characteristics of living things include:

  • Movement
  • Growth
  • Reproduction
  • Response to stimuli
  • Excretion

Examples of living things in Nigeria include:

The African elephant found in the savannas of Northern Nigeria

The Nile tilapia fish found in the rivers of Southern Nigeria

Fun Fact

Did you know that there are over 8.7 million identified living species on Earth, and scientists estimate that there may be up to 30 million more that have not been discovered yet?

Practice Questions

1. What are the basic characteristics of living things?

Answer: Movement, growth, reproduction, response to stimuli, and excretion.

2. Give an example of a living thing found in Nigeria.

Answer: The African elephant or the Nile tilapia fish.

3. What is the estimated number of undiscovered living species on Earth?

Answer: Up to 30 million.
