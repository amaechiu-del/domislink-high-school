Comprehension in English

Comprehension is the ability to understand and interpret written or spoken language. It involves reading or listening to a text, identifying the main ideas, and making connections between them.

To achieve comprehension, follow these steps:

  • Read the text carefully and identify the main ideas
  • Make connections between the ideas
  • Use context clues to understand unfamiliar words or phrases
  • Ask questions to clarify any doubts

For example, in Nigeria, comprehension is crucial when reading news articles about current events, such as the economy or politics. It helps individuals understand the issues and make informed decisions. Another example is when reading Nigerian literature, such as the works of Chinua Achebe or Wole Soyinka, comprehension is essential to appreciate the themes, characters, and plot.

Fun Fact

Did you know that comprehension is not just limited to reading? It also involves listening and understanding spoken language, such as in conversations, speeches, or podcasts.
