Spelling in English (JSS1)

Spelling is an essential aspect of the English language, and it involves the correct arrangement of letters to form words. In this lesson, we will explore the basics of spelling and its importance in effective communication.

Here are some key points to note about spelling:

  • Spelling involves the correct sequence of letters in a word.
  • Good spelling skills are essential for effective communication in writing.
  • Spelling helps to convey meaning and avoid confusion in messages.

Let's consider some Nigerian examples:

For instance, the word "Lagos" is spelled L-A-G-O-S, and the word "Abuja" is spelled A-B-U-J-A. If these words are not spelled correctly, the meaning and location may be lost or misinterpreted.

Fun Fact

Did you know that the English language has many words that are pronounced the same but have different spellings and meanings? These words are called homophones. Examples include "flower" and "flour", or "bare" and "bear".

Now, let's practice what we have learned. Here are three questions to test your spelling skills:

1. What is the correct spelling of the word for a person who teaches?

A) Techer

B) Teacher

C) Techur

Answer: B) Teacher

2. What is the correct spelling of the word for the largest city in Nigeria?

A) Lagos

B) Lagus

C) Lagas

Answer: A) Lagos

3. What is the correct spelling of the word for the capital city of Nigeria?

A) Abija

B) Abuja

C) Abuhja

Answer: B) Abuja
