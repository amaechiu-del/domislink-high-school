Introduction to Statistics

Statistics is the study of the collection, analysis, interpretation, presentation, and organization of data. It involves the use of mathematical techniques to summarize and describe data.

What is Data?

Data refers to the facts and figures collected together for reference or analysis. It can be in the form of numbers, words, images, or videos.

Types of Data

  • Primary data: This is the original data collected by the researcher.
  • Secondary data: This is the data that has been collected by someone else.

Nigerian Examples

For example, the National Population Commission of Nigeria collects data on the population of the country every 10 years. This data is used to plan for the provision of amenities such as schools, hospitals, and roads.

Another example is the data collected by the Nigerian Bureau of Statistics on the country's inflation rate. This data is used to inform policy decisions on the economy.

Fun Fact

Did you know that the word "statistics" comes from the German word "Statistik", which means "study of the state"? This refers to the use of data to understand and describe the state of a country or society.

Practice Questions

Test your understanding of statistics with these questions:

  1. What is the difference between primary and secondary data?
    1. a) Primary data is collected by someone else, while secondary data is original.
    2. b) Primary data is original, while secondary data is collected by someone else.
    3. c) Primary data is used for analysis, while secondary data is used for presentation.
    Answer: b) Primary data is original, while secondary data is collected by someone else.
  2. What is the purpose of collecting data on the population of a country?
    1. a) To plan for the provision of amenities such as schools, hospitals, and roads.
    2. b) To inform policy decisions on the economy.
    3. c) To study the climate and geography of the country.
    Answer: a) To plan for the provision of amenities such as schools, hospitals, and roads.
  3. What does the word "statistics" mean?
    1. a) The study of numbers and figures.
    2. b) The study of the state.
    3. c) The study of data collection methods.
    Answer: b) The study of the state.
