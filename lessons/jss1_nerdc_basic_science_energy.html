Energy

Energy is the ability to do work. It comes in various forms, including kinetic energy, potential energy, thermal energy, electrical energy, and more.

Here are some key points about energy:

  • Energy can be transferred from one form to another, but it cannot be created or destroyed.
  • Energy can be stored in various forms, such as batteries or fuels.
  • Energy is essential for doing work, such as moving objects or powering machines.

Examples of energy in Nigeria include:

  • The Kainji Dam, which generates hydroelectric power for the country.
  • The Egbin Power Plant, which uses thermal energy to produce electricity.

Fun Fact

Did you know that the human body is a great example of energy conversion? The food we eat is converted into chemical energy, which is then used to power our movements and activities.
