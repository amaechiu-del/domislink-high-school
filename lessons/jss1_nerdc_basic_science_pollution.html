Pollution

Pollution is the introduction of harmful substances or products into the environment. It can occur in various forms, including air, water, and land pollution.

Some common causes of pollution include:

  • Industrial waste and emissions
  • Vehicular emissions
  • Agricultural runoff
  • Improper waste disposal

Examples of pollution in Nigeria include:

  • The pollution of the Niger Delta region due to oil spills and gas flaring, which has had devastating effects on the environment and local communities.
  • The pollution of the Lagos Lagoon, which has been caused by a combination of industrial waste, sewage, and agricultural runoff, resulting in the degradation of the water quality and the loss of biodiversity.

Fun Fact

Did you know that pollution is responsible for an estimated 9 million premature deaths worldwide each year? This is more than the number of deaths caused by tuberculosis, malaria, and HIV/AIDS combined.
