Culture in Social Studies

Culture refers to the way of life of a people, including their customs, traditions, and values. It is passed down from one generation to the next and is an essential part of a society's identity.

Key aspects of culture include:

  • Language: the way people communicate with each other
  • Customs: the traditional practices and behaviors of a society
  • Traditions: the beliefs and values that are passed down from one generation to the next

Examples of culture in Nigeria include:

  • The Durbar festival in Kano, which is a traditional horse-riding ceremony
  • The Egungun festival in Oyo, which is a traditional masquerade ceremony

Fun Fact

Did you know that Nigeria has over 250 ethnic groups, each with its own unique culture and traditions?
