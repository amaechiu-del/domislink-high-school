Meaning and Scope of Social Studies

Social Studies is an integrated study of the social sciences and humanities that aims to promote civic competence and social awareness. It encompasses various disciplines, including history, geography, economics, and sociology.

The scope of Social Studies includes:

  • Understanding the social, cultural, and economic aspects of human societies
  • Developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Promoting civic awareness and participation

Examples of Social Studies in Nigeria include:

  • The study of the Hausa-Fulani people and their cultural practices in Northern Nigeria
  • The examination of the impact of oil exploration on the environment and economy of the Niger Delta region

Fun Fact: The term "Social Studies" was first used in the United States in the early 20th century to describe the integration of social science disciplines in the school curriculum.
