Energy

Energy is the ability to do work. It comes in various forms, such as kinetic energy, potential energy, thermal energy, and more.

  • Kinetic energy is the energy of motion. It is the energy an object possesses when it is moving.
  • Potential energy is the energy an object possesses due to its position or state. It can be gravitational potential energy or elastic potential energy.
  • Thermal energy is the energy an object possesses due to the motion of its particles. It is also known as internal energy.

Nigerian examples of energy include:

  • The Kainji Dam, which generates hydroelectric power using the kinetic energy of moving water.
  • The Egbin Power Plant, which generates thermal energy using fossil fuels to produce electricity.

Fun Fact

Did you know that the human body is a great example of energy conversion? The food we eat is converted into chemical energy, which is then converted into kinetic energy, allowing us to move and perform various activities.
