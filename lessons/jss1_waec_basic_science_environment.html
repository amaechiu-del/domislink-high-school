Introduction to Environment

The environment refers to the surrounding in which an organism lives. It includes all the living and non-living things that surround us.

Components of the Environment

  • Air
  • Water
  • Land
  • Living organisms (plants and animals)

Examples of Environmental Conservation in Nigeria

Nigeria has taken steps to conserve its environment. For example:

  • The creation of the Osun Osogbo Sacred Grove, a forest reserve in Osun State, which helps to preserve the natural habitat of various plant and animal species.
  • The establishment of the Old Oyo National Park in Oyo State, which provides a protected area for wildlife and helps to maintain the balance of the ecosystem.

Fun Fact

Did you know that the Niger Delta region in Nigeria is home to a vast array of biodiversity, including mangrove forests, freshwater swamps, and saltwater swamps, which support a wide variety of plant and animal life?
