Living Things

Living things are organisms that have the ability to grow, reproduce, respond to stimuli, and maintain homeostasis. They can be found in various environments, including land, water, and air.

Characteristics of living things include:

  • Ability to grow and develop
  • Ability to reproduce
  • Ability to respond to stimuli
  • Ability to maintain homeostasis

Examples of living things in Nigeria include:

  • Lions in the savannas of Yankari National Park
  • Mangroves in the Niger Delta region

Fun Fact

The largest living thing on Earth is a fungus called Armillaria ostoyae, which covers over 2,200 acres in Oregon, USA.

Practice Questions

1. What is the main characteristic that distinguishes living things from non-living things?

Answer: The ability to grow, reproduce, respond to stimuli, and maintain homeostasis.

2. Give an example of a living thing that can be found in the ocean.

Answer: Fish

3. What is the term for the ability of living things to maintain a stable internal environment?

Answer: Homeostasis
