Introduction to Matter

Matter is anything that has mass and takes up space. It can be found in different states: solid, liquid, and gas.

The three states of matter are:

  • Solid: Has a fixed shape and volume. Examples include rocks, books, and chairs.
  • Liquid: Has a fixed volume but takes the shape of its container. Examples include water, oil, and juice.
  • Gas: Has neither a fixed shape nor a fixed volume. Examples include air, helium, and oxygen.

In Nigeria, we have many examples of matter in different states. For instance:

- The rocks found in Jos, Plateau State are solid matter.

- The water in the Niger Delta is an example of liquid matter.

Fun Fact

Did you know that matter can change from one state to another? This process is called a phase change. For example, when you boil water, it changes from a liquid to a gas.
