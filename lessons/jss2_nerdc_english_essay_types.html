Essay Types

In English, essays can be categorized into several types. Understanding these types is essential for effective communication.

The main types of essays are:

  • Narrative essays: tell a story or recount an experience
  • Descriptive essays: describe a person, place, or thing
  • Expository essays: explain or describe a topic

For example, in Nigeria, a narrative essay could be written about the experience of attending the Osun Osogbo festival, while a descriptive essay could be written about the beauty of the Obudu Plateau.

Another example is an expository essay on the importance of the Niger Delta region in Nigeria's economy.

Fun Fact: The word "essay" comes from the French word "essai," which means "attempt" or "trial."
