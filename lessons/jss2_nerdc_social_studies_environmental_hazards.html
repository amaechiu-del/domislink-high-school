Environmental Hazards

Environmental hazards are harmful events or situations that can cause damage to the environment and human health. They can be natural or man-made.

Some common environmental hazards include:

  • Natural disasters like earthquakes, floods, and droughts
  • Man-made disasters like pollution, deforestation, and climate change

Examples of environmental hazards in Nigeria include:

  • The Boko Haram insurgency in the North-East, which has led to deforestation and pollution in the region
  • The oil spills in the Niger Delta, which have caused significant damage to the environment and human health

Fun Fact

Did you know that Nigeria is one of the countries most vulnerable to climate change? Rising temperatures and changing rainfall patterns are affecting agriculture, water resources, and human health in the country.
