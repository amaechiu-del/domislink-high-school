Labor Unions

Labor unions are organizations that represent the interests of workers in a particular industry or profession. They aim to improve working conditions, wages, and benefits for their members.

The main goals of labor unions include:

  • Negotiating better wages and benefits for workers
  • Improving working conditions and safety standards
  • Protecting workers' rights and interests

In Nigeria, examples of labor unions include:

  • Nigeria Labour Congress (NLC)
  • Trade Union Congress of Nigeria (TUC)

Fun Fact

The first labor union in Nigeria was formed in 1912 by Nigerian railway workers.
