Introduction to Government

Government refers to the system or group of people that have the authority to make and enforce decisions for a country, state, or organization.

The main functions of government include:

  • Maintaining law and order
  • Providing public services
  • Collecting taxes
  • Regulating economic activities

In Nigeria, there are three tiers of government: Federal, State, and Local. For example:

  • The Federal Government is responsible for the overall governance of the country, while the State Government is responsible for the governance of each state. For instance, the Federal Government is responsible for the Nigerian Army, while the State Government is responsible for the State Traffic Management Authority.
  • The Local Government is responsible for providing basic amenities such as water, roads, and healthcare at the local level. For example, the Lagos State Local Government is responsible for managing the waste disposal system in Lagos.

Fun Fact

The word "government" comes from the Latin word "gubernare," which means "to direct" or "to govern."

Test your understanding of government with these practice questions:

  1. What is the main purpose of government?

  2. Which tier of government is responsible for providing basic amenities such as water and roads?
    1. Federal Government
    2. State Government
    3. Local Government
    Answer: c) Local Government
  3. What is the name of the Latin word from which the word "government" is derived?
    1. Gubernare
    2. Governare
    3. Directare
    Answer: a) Gubernare
