Introduction to Geometry

Geometry is the study of shapes, sizes, and positions of objects. It involves the use of points, lines, and angles to describe and analyze these objects.

In this lesson, we will be exploring the basics of geometry, including points, lines, and angles.

  • A point is a location in space, represented by a set of coordinates.
  • A line is a set of points that extend infinitely in two directions.
  • An angle is formed by two lines that intersect at a point.

Practice Questions

1. What is the definition of a point in geometry?

Answer: A point is a location in space, represented by a set of coordinates.

2. What is the difference between a line and a ray?

Answer: A line extends infinitely in two directions, while a ray extends infinitely in one direction.

3. What is the sum of the interior angles of a triangle?

Answer: 180 degrees.
