ЁЯУМ What is a Frequency Table?

  • A frequency table organizes data by showing how often each value occurs.
  • It has two columns: Score (or Class) and Frequency.
  • It helps to quickly see the most common, least common, and spread of data.
  • We can calculate mean, median, mode, and range from a frequency table.

ЁЯУЭ Practice Questions

  1. Question 1: The scores of 10 students in a quiz are: 1, 2, 2, 3, 3, 3, 4, 4, 5, 5. Draw a frequency table and find the modal score.
  2. Question 2: In a market survey, 12 women bought the following numbers of yams: 2, 2, 3, 3, 3, 4, 4, 4, 4, 5, 5, 6. Create a frequency table and find the range.
  3. Question 3: The ages of 8 children in a family are: 5, 6, 6, 7, 7, 7, 8, 9. Use a frequency table to find the mean age.

тЬЕ Answers

  • Answer 1: Modal score = 3 (appears 3 times). Table: Score 1 (1), 2 (2), 3 (3), 4 (2), 5 (2). Total = 10.
  • Answer 2: Range = 6 - 2 = 4. Table: 2 (2), 3 (3), 4 (4), 5 (2), 6 (1). Total = 12.
  • Answer 3: Mean = (5├Ч1 + 6├Ч2 + 7├Ч3 + 8├Ч1 + 9├Ч1) ├╖ 8 = (5+12+21+8+9) ├╖ 8 = 55 ├╖ 8 = 6.875.
