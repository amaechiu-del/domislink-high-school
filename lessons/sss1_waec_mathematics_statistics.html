Statistics: Introduction to Data and Frequency

Key Concepts in Statistics

  • Data: Information collected in the form of numbers, words, or measurements (e.g., ages, test scores).
  • Frequency: How often a particular value or category occurs in a dataset.
  • Frequency Table: A table that shows each data value alongside its frequency.
  • Bar Chart: A visual representation using bars to compare frequencies of different categories.
  • Mean: The average of a set of numbers (sum ÷ count).
  • Median: The middle value when data is arranged in order.
  • Mode: The value that appears most frequently.

Nigerian Examples

Example 1: A market woman in Lagos recorded the number of yams sold each day for a week: Monday: 10, Tuesday: 15, Wednesday: 10, Thursday: 20, Friday: 15, Saturday: 25, Sunday: 5. Find the mean, median, and mode.

Solution:
Mean = (10+15+10+20+15+25+5) ÷ 7 = 100 ÷ 7 ≈ 14.3 yams per day.
Sorted data: 5, 10, 10, 15, 15, 20, 25 → Median = 15 (4th value).
Mode = 10 and 15 (both appear twice).

Example 2: A teacher in Abuja recorded the scores of 10 students in a Mathematics test: 12, 15, 18, 12, 20, 15, 12, 10, 18, 15. Create a frequency table and find the mode.

Solution:
Frequency table: 10 (1), 12 (3), 15 (3), 18 (2), 20 (1).
Mode = 12 and 15 (each appears 3 times).

🌟 Fun Fact: Did you know? Statistics were used in ancient Nigeria for census and tax collection during the Benin Empire! The Oba (king) kept records of households and farm produce to plan for the kingdom.

Practice Questions

  1. The ages (in years) of 8 students in an SSS1 class are: 14, 15, 14, 16, 15, 14, 17, 15. What is the mean age?
  2. Find the median of the following scores: 8, 12, 5, 9, 12, 7, 10.
  3. In a survey of 20 families in Port Harcourt, the number of children per family was: 2, 3, 1, 4, 2, 3, 2, 1, 2, 3, 4, 2, 3, 2, 1, 2, 3, 2, 4, 2. What is the mode?

Answers

  • Answer 1: Mean = (14+15+14+16+15+14+17+15) ÷ 8 = 120 ÷ 8 = 15 years.
  • Answer 2: Sorted data: 5, 7, 8, 9, 10, 12, 12 → Median = 9 (4th value).
  • Answer 3: Mode = 2 (appears 9 times).
