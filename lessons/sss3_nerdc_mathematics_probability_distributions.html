Key Concepts

  • Probability Distribution — A function that gives the probability of each possible outcome of a random variable.
  • Discrete Distribution — For countable outcomes (e.g., number of students, coin tosses).
  • Continuous Distribution — For outcomes on a continuous scale (e.g., height, time).
  • Properties: Each probability is between 0 and 1, and the sum of all probabilities equals 1.
  • Mean (Expected Value) — Weighted average: μ = Σ x·P(x)
  • Variance — Measure of spread: σ² = Σ (x−μ)²·P(x)

🇳🇬 Nigerian Examples

Example 1: JAMB Exam Scores
In a class of 40 SSS3 students, the number of students who scored above 250 in JAMB is a random variable X. If P(X=10)=0.2, P(X=15)=0.5, P(X=20)=0.3, find the expected number of students scoring above 250.
Solution: μ = 10(0.2) + 15(0.5) + 20(0.3) = 2 + 7.5 + 6 = 15.5 students
Example 2: Lagos Traffic Light
A traffic light at Ikeja shows red for 40% of the time, yellow 10%, green 50%. If X = colour (1=red, 2=yellow, 3=green), find P(X is not red).
Solution: P(not red) = P(yellow) + P(green) = 0.10 + 0.50 = 0.60
💡 Fun Fact: The binomial distribution was first studied by Swiss mathematician Jakob Bernoulli in the 1600s. Today, it's used by the Nigerian National Bureau of Statistics to predict election outcomes and by telecom companies like MTN to model network failures!

📝 Practice Questions

  1. A dice is rolled. Let X be the number shown. What is P(X is even)?
  2. The probability distribution of a random variable Y is: P(Y=0)=0.1, P(Y=1)=0.3, P(Y=2)=0.4, P(Y=3)=0.2. Calculate the variance of Y.
  3. In a Nigerian school, 60% of students own a smartphone. If 5 students are chosen at random, what is the probability that exactly 3 own a smartphone? (Use binomial formula)

✅ Answers

  1. P(even) = P(2)+P(4)+P(6) = 1/6+1/6+1/6 = 1/2
  2. Mean μ = 0(0.1)+1(0.3)+2(0.4)+3(0.2)=1.7; Variance = (0-1.7)²·0.1 + (1-1.7)²·0.3 + (2-1.7)²·0.4 + (3-1.7)²·0.2 = 2.89·0.1 + 0.49·0.3 + 0.09·0.4 + 1.69·0.2 = 0.289+0.147+0.036+0.338 = 0.81
  3. Using binomial: n=5, p=0.6, q=0.4, k=3 → P = ⁵C₃ (0.6)³(0.4)² = 10 × 0.216 × 0.16 = 0.3456
